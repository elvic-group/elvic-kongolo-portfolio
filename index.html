About Elvic Kongolo

Founder & CEO | Cultural Entrepreneur | Music Producer

I'm a social entrepreneur bridging culture, tourism, and community development across Norway and DR Congo. Since 2017, I've founded four ventures that have delivered cultural experiences to over 500 participants across 8 municipalities in Northern Norway, with extensive media coverage in over 32 national and regional publications.

What I Do

  • Kulturbryggeriet & Nord på Hjul (Founded 2022)

    Cultural production company delivering mobile roller-skating disco events across Northern Norway. Since 2022, we've produced over 20 events in 8 municipalities including Hammerfest, Tromsø, Berlevåg, Øksnes, Lofoten, Sortland, Kvalsund, and Laukvik, engaging over 500 participants. Our work creates accessible cultural experiences that bridge communities and promote inclusion.

    Supported by: Samfunnsløftet Sparebank, Tromsø Kommune, Bufdir, Finnmark Fylkeskommune, and other partners.

  • Elvic Gruppen AS (Founded 2024)

    Hospitality and tourism development company supporting regional economic growth through innovative ventures in the hotel and restaurant sector. Launched in 2024 with positive reception from media and communities in Hammerfest and Berlevåg. Our goal is to expand the concept across Norway.

  • Elvic Adventures (Founded 2024)

    Outdoor tourism operator offering water biking and guided nature experiences across Northern Norway. Launched in 2024 with strong community support and media coverage, we're expanding to make Northern Norway's unique landscapes accessible through creative tourism experiences for all ages.

  • Musikkbryggeriet (Founded 2017)

    Music industry development program connecting emerging Norwegian and international artists with industry professionals. Since 2017, we've delivered:

    • Major events: Lembe Concert (climate awareness), Musikkfest på Grønland, Oslo Kulturnatt på Tøyen, Grønlandfest, Tøyen Julemarked, Månefestival collaboration
    • Educational programs: Songwriting camps, vocal training with industry professionals like Eric Elvenes and Tracy Meen, music production courses for youth and seniors, Q&A sessions on music rights and promotion

Background

Born in DR Congo, I moved to Norway in 2009 after beginning a bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Informatics at Université pédagogique de Kinshasa. As a performing artist and music producer, I've appeared at major Norwegian festivals including Moldejazz, Slottsfjell, and Mela, and performed at the 2016 Youth Olympics in Lillehammer after winning a P3 Urørt award.

My singles "Let's Get Together" and "Likelele" (featuring Stella Mwangi) received national radio play and critical acclaim on P3 Urørt, Norway's premier platform for emerging artists.
